About

The Edit Serves The Story

Jotham Cuts is a self-taught video editor specialising in retention-focused long-form YouTube content. Working entirely in DaVinci Resolve, the approach is simple: every cut is intentional, nothing stays by accident, and the viewer never gets a reason to leave.

With a sharp eye for pacing and a deep focus on sound design, the goal is always to make the audience feel present — not like they're watching a highlight reel, but like they're actually there. From outdoor discovery and rockhounding content to hobby and adventure channels, the edit serves the story. Not the other way around.

Free sample edits available for first-time clients. No commitment. No risk. Just an easy way to see if the style fits before either of us decides anything.
